HTML Headings: Structure and Significance

HTML headings, denoted by ""h1" to "h6" tags, establish a hierarchical structure for your web page's content, conveying its organization and importance to both users and search engines.


  • Levels
    • h1: The most important heading, typically used for the main title of the page. There should only be one h1 per page.
    h2 to h6: Subsequent subheadings, progressively decreasing in significance.
