Exploring Basic HTML Styling

Styling Methods

HTML offers three main approaches to style your content:

  • Inline Styles (using the style attribute): Applied directly to individual elements. (Lowest Priority)
  • Internal Styles (using the style tag): Defined within the `head` section of the document. (Medium Priority)
  • External Styles (using a separate `.css` file): Linked to the HTML document using the `link` tag. (Highest Priority)
